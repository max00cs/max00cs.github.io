The Secret of the "Pfefferl"

The Weinviertel (Wine Quarter) is located in northeastern Lower Austria. Why are the wines here so special? The unique combination of cool night breezes and loess soil gives the local Grüner Veltliner a distinct, refreshing white-pepper spice known locally as the "Pfefferl".
