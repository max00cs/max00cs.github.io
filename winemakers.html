The Masters of the Vines

Meet some dedicated families who have shaped Weinviertel winery generations.

Weingut Müllner

📍 Dürnleis, Northern Weinviertel

The Müllner family are not only close friends to my family but also an integral part of the Weinviertel wine community. They focus on organic wine production and sustainable practices.

Signature Wine: Welschriesling

Weingut Hagn

📍 Mailberg, Northern Weinviertel

The Hagn family has been producing wine for over 300 years. They are renowned for their wines and commitment to the local community.

Signature Wine: Grüner Veltliner Ried Hundschupfen

Weingut Setzer

📍 Hohenwarth, Western Weinviertel

With vine cuttings dating back to 1705, the Setzer family cultivates high-altitude vineyards. They specialize in both Grüner Veltliner and the rare, indigenous Roter Veltliner grape.

Signature Wine: Grüner Veltliner 8000
